Examples of Triangles

A Triangle is a simple curved or polygon formed by three lines-segments

Properties of the Triangles

Equilateral:

  1. All three sides and three angles are equal
    2. The three angles are 60 degrees each.

Scalene:

  1. The sides and angles are not common amongst them.

Isoceles:

  1. Isosceles triangle has two sides, b and c, and the two angles opposite them B and C equal. The third angle, A, could be acute or obtuse.
